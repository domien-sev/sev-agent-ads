# Ad Copy Generation Prompt

Generate ad copy for fashion outlet products.

## Format per platform

### Meta (Facebook/Instagram)
- Primary text: 125 chars max
- Headline: 40 chars max
- Description: 30 chars max
- CTA: SHOP_NOW, LEARN_MORE, GET_OFFER

### Google (Responsive Search/Display)
- Headline: 30 chars max (up to 15 headlines)
- Description: 90 chars max (up to 4 descriptions)
- Path: 15 chars per segment

### TikTok
- Ad text: 100 chars recommended
- Casual, trend-aware tone
- Use emojis sparingly

### Pinterest
- Title: 100 chars max
- Description: 500 chars max
- Aspirational, lifestyle-focused
